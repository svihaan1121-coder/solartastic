Mercury

Module 01

Core Data

1. High-Velocity Orbital Mechanics

Mercury orbits the Sun at an average distance of roughly 36 million miles (58 million km). It travels at nearly 29 miles per second (47 km/s). Its year is only 88 Earth days long.

2. The Mystery of the Giant Core

Mercury's iron core is massive, occupying about 85% of the planet's radius.

3. Extreme Thermal Dynamics

Day surface reaches 800°F (430°C). Night temperature drops to -290°F (-180°C).

4. 3:2 Spin-Orbit Resonance

Mercury rotates three times for every two orbits around the Sun. A single day-night cycle takes 176 Earth days.
